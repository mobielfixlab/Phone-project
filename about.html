About us

Repair is a craft.
We treat it like one.

Mobile Fix Lab was built on a simple frustration: too many shops swap parts and hope. We find the real fault, fix it with care, and stand behind our work.

Our story

From one bench to a full lab.

What started as one technician and a workbench has grown into a full repair lab handling everything from cracked screens to water-damaged laptops.

The philosophy never changed: find the real fault, fix only what's broken, and charge honestly for it. That's why most of our new customers come from word of mouth.

Every technician here trains for careful, precise repair — not quick part swaps. It takes us longer to learn. It saves you money.

How we work

Four steps.
Zero guesswork.

Diagnose

Thorough diagnostics — free on most repairs. We find the actual fault, not just the visible symptom, and give you a clear quote before any work starts.

Repair

Quality parts fitted with care. From a 30-minute battery swap to a full screen replacement, the same precision applies to every job on the bench.

Test

Every function checked before hand-back: display, touch, cameras, speakers, sensors, charging at full current. If it's not perfect, it doesn't leave the lab.

Warranty

90 days on parts and workmanship for most repairs, no fine print gymnastics. If something we fixed fails, we make it right — fast and free.

The standard

Slow hands. Fast turnaround.

What we stand for

Honest by default.

Fix, don't replace

If a small fix solves it, we won't sell you an expensive part you don't need. Repair-first is cheaper for you and better for the planet.

Respect your time

Clear time estimates, live updates when you want them, and same-day turnaround on the most common repairs.

Privacy first
