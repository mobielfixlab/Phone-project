Areas we serve

One lab. Six towns.

Our repair lab sits at 11205 Lawyers Rd Suite A in Mint Hill — within an easy drive of Matthews, southeast Charlotte, Indian Trail, Stallings and Harrisburg. Same-day repairs, walk-ins welcome Mon–Sat.

Mint Hill, NC

Home base. The lab is on Lawyers Rd, minutes from downtown Mint Hill — drop your device off, grab lunch nearby, and most screens and batteries are ready within the hour.

Matthews, NC

About 10 minutes from Matthews via NC-51 or Idlewild Rd. Plenty of Matthews neighbors drop devices on their commute and pick them up the same evening.

Charlotte, NC

Easy to reach from southeast Charlotte — Idlewild, Hickory Grove, and anywhere along Lawyers Rd or Albemarle Rd. Faster in and out than fighting Uptown traffic for a mall kiosk.

Indian Trail, NC

Roughly 15 minutes away. If your phone, tablet or console needs fixing, it's a straight shot up from Indian Trail — call ahead and we'll have the part ready.

Stallings, NC

Right next door — about 10 minutes from most of Stallings. Same-day turnaround on most repairs means you're rarely without your device overnight.

Harrisburg, NC

Around 20 minutes from Harrisburg. Worth the short drive for board-level repairs — water damage, HDMI ports and micro-soldering that most shops send away.

A bit farther out? We've had devices come from all over the Charlotte metro — call us or request a quote and we'll sort out the details.
