FAQ

Questions we hear every day

How long does a phone screen repair take?

Most phone screen replacements are finished in about 45 minutes. Batteries usually take around 30 minutes, and nearly all phone repairs are completed the same day.

Do I need an appointment?

No — walk-ins are welcome Monday through Saturday, 10 AM to 7 PM. Booking online just guarantees your parts are on the bench before you arrive.

Is there a warranty on repairs?

Most repairs are backed by a 90-day parts and labor warranty — we'll tell you exactly what's covered with your quote. If anything related to the repair goes wrong, bring it back and we'll make it right.

Will I lose my data during a repair?

No. Screen, battery and port repairs never touch your data, and we don't need your passcode for most jobs. Your device and data stay private and in-house.

How much will my repair cost?

Diagnostics are always free. You get a firm quote up front and approve it before we open your device — iPhone screens start at $79 and batteries at $69.
