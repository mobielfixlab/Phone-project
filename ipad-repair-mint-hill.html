What we fix on iPads & tablets

  • Cracked glass & touch problems — shattered corners, spidered glass, unresponsive touch
  • Display faults — black screen, lines, flickering or backlight problems
  • Batteries — draining fast, not holding charge, or swollen (screen lifting)
  • Charging ports — loose cables, slow charging, no charge at all
  • Buttons, speakers & cameras — stuck buttons, muffled audio, blurry photos

Glass or screen? It changes the price

On many iPads the touch glass (digitizer) and the display underneath are separate parts. If the glass is cracked but the picture is perfect, you often only need the cheaper glass repair. If the image itself is damaged, the display needs replacing too. We diagnose it for free and quote both clearly — screens start at $79.

Why tablets take a little longer than phones

Tablet glass is bonded to the frame with strong adhesive that has to be heated, separated and cleaned before a new panel can be fitted properly — rushing this step is how corners lift and dust gets sealed in. Most repairs are still same-day or within 24 hours, and every device is fully tested before pickup.

FAQ

iPad & tablet repair questions

How much does iPad screen repair cost?

How long does an iPad repair take?

Most are done same-day or within 24 hours — the bonded glass needs careful separation and clean re-bonding, which we won't rush.

Do you repair Samsung and Android tablets?

Yes — Samsung Galaxy Tab, Lenovo, Amazon Fire and most others, including kids' tablets. Screens, batteries, ports and buttons.

Is an older iPad worth repairing?

Usually yes — iPads stay useful for years and a repair is far cheaper than replacing one. If it's not worth it for your model, we'll tell you straight.
