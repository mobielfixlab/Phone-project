What decides your exact price

Two things: the model, and the panel grade. Newer OLED iPhones cost more to fix than older LCD models, and screens come in grades — premium aftermarket and genuine service-pack panels. We tell you which grade you're getting and price both where available, so the quote you approve is the price you pay. For the full breakdown, see our screen repair cost guide.
