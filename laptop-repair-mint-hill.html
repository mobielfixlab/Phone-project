FAQ

Laptop repair questions

How much does laptop screen repair cost?

From $99 for MacBooks and Windows laptops, depending on the panel. You get a firm written quote before any work starts.

How long does a laptop repair take?

Screens typically take 1–2 days depending on part availability. Batteries, keyboards and upgrades are often same or next day; liquid damage treatment runs 24–48 hours.

Is my data safe?

Yes — your data stays on your drive and never leaves your machine. Most repairs don't need your password, and we ask first when testing does.

Can you make my slow laptop faster?

Usually — an SSD and RAM upgrade transforms most older laptops for far less than a new machine. We'll tell you honestly if it isn't worth it for your model.

I spilled liquid on my MacBook — what now?

Power off, unplug, don't charge it, and bring it in fast — corrosion starts within hours, and quick ultrasonic cleaning saves most liquid-damaged laptops.
